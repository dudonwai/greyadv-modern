PetroRecruit

Set on becoming the LinkedIn for Oil and Gas in Canada, GreyAdv worked with PetroRecruit to build an online marketplace specifically for job hunters and employers on the Canadian petroleum industry. Surprisingly, there are still open jobs in the industry - if you know where to look. GreyAdv helped develop a workflow product that brings all job postings to one site, and helps job seekers and employers search and filter for the right match.
