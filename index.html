Our Curriculm

Our diverse & practical workshops cover all you need to know.

Web Design

Only 31% of small businesses in canada have a website, we believe being in this demographic is crutial to running a sucessful SMB. Our experts teach you wordpress, which is an accessible and convient way to get your business online.

Minimum Viable Product

MVP (or minimum viable product) is part of the Lean Startup philosophy. Creating an MVP, means using minimum effort to form a testable business idea. We want to apply MVP theory to turn your ideas into something tangible that you can build a business on.
